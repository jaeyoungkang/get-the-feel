# Engineering Rules

- L0 is the default for R1: asset check, data contract check, syntax check.
- Do not add lint, knip, duplication, purity, build, or CI until an engineering trigger fires.
- Engineering escalation uses `../shared-skills/engineering-decision/SKILL.md` and references `https://github.com/jaeyoungkang/agentic-base.git`.
- Candidate folders should remain independently openable static apps until a dependency trigger fires.
