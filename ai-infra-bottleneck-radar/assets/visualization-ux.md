# Visualization And UX

## R1 Surface Hypothesis

A radial pressure radar makes bottleneck severity and propagation more immediately visible than a conventional dashboard.

## Visual Grammar

- Center: AI infrastructure demand.
- Ring nodes: value-chain stages.
- Thick colored arcs: propagation pressure.
- Highlighted path: active thin path for source/freshness inspection.
- Side panel: micro evidence and operating contract, not the primary surface.

## Reject Patterns

- Four KPI cards as the main surface.
- Company comparison table as the main surface.
- Source/legal text that hides the map.
- Scenario labels that look like price forecasts.
