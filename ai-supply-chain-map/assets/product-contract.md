# Product Contract

## Buyer

Ordinary stock investors who want to understand AI infrastructure supply pressure before reading company-specific news or earnings.

## Core Promise

Within 30 seconds, the user can identify the most pressured AI supply-chain bottleneck, see where that pressure propagates, and drill from macro stage to micro evidence without turning the product into a stock recommendation tool.

## Refusals

- No buy/sell/target-price advice.
- No single-stock card dashboard as the primary surface.
- No future snapshot labeled as observed fact.
- No observed label for example, unknown, or unverified values.

## Acceptance

- The first viewport is a macro supply-chain map.
- Bottleneck pressure is visible through position, size, color, or edge weight.
- Clicking a macro node reveals micro evidence.
- Time snapshots distinguish observed, scenario, and unverified states.
