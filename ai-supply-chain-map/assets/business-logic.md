# Business Logic And Sellability

## Sellability Gate

Every cycle after the first prototype must answer:

- `buyer`
- `paid_job`
- `pricing_hypothesis`
- `freshness_promise`
- `update_owner`
- `data_cost_or_manual_work`
- `release_blockers`
- `sales_risk_verdict`

## Current Hypothesis

The paid job is not stock prediction. It is reducing time-to-understanding for AI infrastructure supply bottlenecks before investors read earnings, capex news, or supplier commentary.

## Current Blockers

- Real source pipeline not implemented.
- Data licensing and update cost unknown.
- Example scores are not sellable as observed data.
