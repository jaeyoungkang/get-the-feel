# Process And Monitor Recovery

## Required Monitors

- Intent Guardian: protects the original macro-map investor ask.
- Process Improvement Monitor: checks gates, templates, and mechanical verdict gaps.
- Asset Steward Monitor: checks whether reusable assets, including product-specific skills, actually grew.
- Data Operations Monitor: required while update cadence, time change, and future snapshots are part of the promise.

## Recovery Rule

Monitor output must be moved into one of:

- `skills/`
- `assets/`
- cycle record template or gate
- mechanical check
- product code/data contract

Feedback stored only as a transcript does not close a cycle.
