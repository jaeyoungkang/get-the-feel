# Product Contract

## Target User

Ordinary stock investors who follow AI infrastructure companies but need a macro view before reading company-specific news.

## Core Promise

Within 30 seconds, the user can say:

- which part of the AI value chain is under the most pressure,
- how that pressure moves downstream or upstream,
- what micro evidence to watch next,
- whether the view is source-backed current index or future scenario.

## Refusals

- No buy/sell/target-price signal.
- No single-stock dashboard as the primary surface.
- No future snapshot presented as fact.
- No `observed` label for curated/example/model values.

## Acceptance

- First viewport is a macro value-chain flow map.
- Bottleneck pressure is visible through node size/color and edge weight.
- Clicking a node exposes micro evidence and source contract.
- Time controls change the same map and clearly label source-backed vs scenario.
